    • Clearing imports from China to South Africa?

  • · The cargo that arrives in South Africa from China has to go through certain procedures before getting into the country.
  • · The South African Revenue Service is responsible for seeing that the goods are under regulations.
  • · It takes care of all the customs requirements and regulations of South Africa.
  • · The customs administration of South Africa plays an important role to facilitate the movements across the borders of South Africa.
  • · The SARS use electronic data in the submission of certain types of data and reports to the relevant authorities.
  • · Anybody who is interested in doing any business with SARS has to register with the EDI.
  • · SARS will offer one administrative document to help in the facilitation of customs procedures.
  • · The procedure to follow before admitting goods in South Africa is almost the same worldwide.
    • There are three main procedures that you will have to follow when importing the goods.

    The procedures are:

  • Register and fill in the forms that will identify you as an importer
  • Get the specific license that you will need for the importation of the goods.
  • Getting the certificates and clearance from the countries, you seek to get the goods.


    • How long does it take to ship from China to South Africa?

  • · The time it will take for your goods to arrive in South Africa will depend on the type of transport.
  • · Air transport is faster than sea transport and will take less time in comparison to sea freights.
  • · The time at sea will also depend on the sea route or the distance to cover and speed of the ship.
